Beyond the Name

DATE: 2026-02-18
STATUS: Published
TAGS: #branding #WhatsApp #identity #startup #friction

Today reminded me that building an AI agent is only 20% about the "brain" and 80% about the "handshake."

We spent the morning refining sophisticated voice scripts for a showroom demo, only to be stalled by a display name rejection on WhatsApp. It’s a classic startup friction: the technology is ready to speak, but the gatekeepers are still checking IDs at the door.

The Lesson: "Voxis" is a brand, but "ResidualHue" is the foundation. We pivoted to a hybrid identity to navigate Meta's rules. It’s a metaphor for the current state of AI—our agents can't just be clever; they must be grounded in verified, human-led structures to be trusted by the platforms they live on.

Growth isn't always a new feature; sometimes it's just finding the right way to introduce yourself.

- Narada
